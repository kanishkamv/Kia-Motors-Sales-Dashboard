# 🚗 Kia Motors Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Kia Motors Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze vehicle sales performance from **2020 to 2024**. The dashboard provides executives and business stakeholders with a comprehensive view of revenue, unit sales, regional performance, product performance, and year-over-year growth through dynamic and interactive visualizations.

The project demonstrates the use of **Power BI Desktop**, **Power Query**, and **DAX** to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Businesses require a centralized dashboard to monitor sales performance across different regions, cities, and vehicle models. Analyzing sales manually is time-consuming and makes it difficult to identify trends and growth opportunities.

This dashboard solves the problem by providing:

- Interactive sales monitoring
- Regional revenue analysis
- Model-wise performance analysis
- Revenue trend visualization
- Year-over-Year (YoY) growth tracking
- Executive-level KPI reporting

---

# 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains Kia Motors sales information collected between **2020 and 2024**.

### Dataset includes:

- Order Date
- Year
- Region
- City
- Vehicle Model
- Vehicle Segment
- Units Sold
- Revenue
- Average Revenue per Unit

The data was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query Editor** before building the dashboard.

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides an overall summary of sales performance.

### Features

- Total Revenue
- Average Revenue per Unit
- Total Units Sold
- Best Performing Year
- Revenue Trend (2020–2024)
- Revenue by Region
- Interactive Year, Region, and Model filters

---

## 2️⃣ Model Performance Analysis

Analyzes sales and revenue across different Kia vehicle models.

### Visuals Included

- Units Sold by Model
- Revenue Share by Vehicle Segment
- Revenue by Model

This page helps identify the best-performing vehicle models and market segments.

---

## 3️⃣ Regional Analysis

Provides location-based sales insights.

### Visuals Included

- Revenue by Zone
- Units Sold by City
- Revenue Comparison across Regions

This enables stakeholders to understand geographical sales performance.

---

## 4️⃣ Growth Analysis

Measures business growth across different years.

### Visuals Included

- Year-over-Year Growth KPIs
- Revenue Waterfall Chart
- Model Growth Comparison
- Growth Trend Analysis

This page highlights revenue growth patterns and compares yearly business performance.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Revenue increased consistently from **2020 to 2024**, indicating steady business growth.
- **South Region** generated the highest revenue among all sales regions.
- **Sonet** emerged as the highest-performing vehicle model in terms of units sold.
- SUV vehicles contributed the largest share of total revenue.
- Revenue growth remained positive across multiple years, demonstrating stable market demand.
- City-level analysis identified the strongest-performing markets for future business expansion.
